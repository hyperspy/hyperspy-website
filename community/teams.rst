Committee and Teams
===================

Steering Committee
------------------

The HyperSpy Steering Committee is responsible for the overall direction and governance
of the project. It is made up of representatives from the core development teams and the
broader community.

In order to improve diversity and representation in the steering committee, the steering
committee will create roles for community representatives.

Core developers Teams
---------------------

The core developers teams of the HyperSpy eco-system are listed publicly here. Each team is
responsible for the maintenance and development of the respective packages.

.. note::

  If the composition of a team is outdated, please open a pull request to update the team
  information at https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy-website.
